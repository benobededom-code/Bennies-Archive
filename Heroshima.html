Hero-shima

Meet the mascots and heroes of Hiroshima sports.

Sanfrecce Hiroshima

Team Information

Sanfrecce Hiroshima is one of the most successful football clubs in Japan's J-League. They have won three league titles in their history in 2012, 2013 and 2015.

The hybridname "Sanfrecce" combines the Japanese words for "three" and italian word frecce for "arrows."

Hiroshima Dragonflies

Team Information

Hiroshima Dragonflies compete in the B.League, Japan's top professional basketball league.

The dragonfly symbolizes strength, determination and speed.

Hiroshima Toyo Carp

Team Information

Hiroshima Toyo Carp is one of Japan's most beloved baseball teams.

The team is famous for its passionate fans and strong community support.
